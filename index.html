Process Flow

How It Works

  1. Collection

    Plastic waste collection and pre‑sorting of incoming streams.

  2. Dissolution Bath

    Room‑temperature dissolution using bio‑compatible amine solvents.

  3. Enzymatic Treatment

    Selective enzyme action for mixed plastics with high activity.

  4. Monomer Recovery

    Separation and capture of monomers for re‑polymerization.

  5. Purification

    Refinement to virgin‑quality feedstock suitable for packaging.

Technical Approach

Technical Approach

Bio‑Compatible Solvents

Room‑temperature dissolution with amines like ethylenediamine and propanediamine—halogen‑free, low toxicity, high selectivity.

Enzyme‑Assisted Breakdown

Dual‑enzyme systems with up to 10,000× activity for mixed plastic streams without degrading quality.

Energy Efficiency

3–10× lower energy consumption than thermal chemical recycling (no 400–700°C stage).

Circular Solvent Loop

95% solvent recovery through closed‑loop purification and reuse for near‑zero waste operations.
