How ECO-DISSOLVE Works

Bio‑compatible amines at room temperature plus advanced enzymes enable selective breakdown of plastic polymers into high‑value monomers.

  1. 1

    Collection & Pre‑sorting

    Mixed and contaminated plastic streams are collected; light pre‑sorting only—no intensive multi‑polymer separation.

  2. 2

    Bio‑Compatible Dissolution

    Plastics are dissolved at room temperature using biocompatible amines (e.g., ethylenediamine, propanediamine), minimizing energy and eliminating harsh chemicals.

  3. 3

    Enzymatic Treatment

    Dual‑enzyme systems (10,000× activity) selectively cleave polymer bonds, enabling high‑efficiency depolymerization even in mixed streams.

  4. 4

    Monomer Recovery & Purification

    Recovered monomers are purified to virgin quality; solvents are reclaimed in a 95% circular loop, ready for reuse.

  5. 5

    Virgin‑Quality Outputs

    Outputs suitable for food‑grade packaging and high‑value industrial applications—turning plastic waste into wealth.

Scientific Foundation

  • JBEI/LBNL: 95% PET depolymerization at room temperature
  • Actinobacteria‑derived amines; halogen‑free; minimal impact
  • Advanced enzyme systems for mixed plastic processing

Process Advantages

  • 3–10× lower energy than 400–700°C thermal routes
  • Handles contaminated and mixed streams
  • 95% solvent recovery; zero harsh chemical discharge
