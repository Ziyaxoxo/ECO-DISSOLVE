Process Flow

How ECO-DISSOLVE Works

A safe, selective route from plastic waste to virgin-quality monomers.

  1. 1

    Plastic Collection & Pre‑Sorting

    Mixed and contaminated streams accepted; minimal sorting required.

  2. 2

    Bio‑Compatible Dissolution

    Room‑temperature amines (e.g., ethylenediamine, propanediamine) dissolve polymers safely.

  3. 3

    Enzyme‑Assisted Breakdown

    Selective, highly active enzymes (10,000×) target polymer bonds without quality loss.

  4. 4

    Monomer Recovery & Purification

    Recover and purify monomers; solvents are recaptured for reuse in a closed loop.

  5. 5

    Virgin‑Quality Materials

    Outputs suitable for food‑grade packaging and high‑value applications.
