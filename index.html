Process Flow

How ECO-DISSOLVE Works

From mixed, contaminated plastics to valuable monomers and materials

  1. 1

    Plastic Collection

    Municipal, mixed, and contaminated plastic streams gathered from landfills and communities.

  2. 2

    Pre‑sorting

    Basic separation without complex sorting; system handles mixed streams.

  3. 3

    Bio‑Compatible Dissolution

    Amines such as ethylenediamine and propanediamine dissolve polymers at room temperature.

  4. 4

    Enzymatic Treatment

    Selective enzymes accelerate depolymerization without degrading quality.

  5. 5

    Monomer Recovery

    Recovered monomers are purified with a 95% solvent recovery loop.

  6. 6

    Virgin‑Quality Output

    Food‑grade, high‑value materials sold back to manufacturers — waste becomes wealth.
