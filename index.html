How It Works

Step 1

Plastic waste is collected and sorted using efficient waste management systems.

Step 2

The ECO-DISSOLVE chemical solution is applied, breaking down plastics at the molecular level.

Step 3

The plastic is permanently dissolved into harmless compounds, eliminating landfill and ocean leakage.

Step 4

By-products are safely processed, contributing to renewable resources and circular economy goals.

Technical Approach

Biocompatible Solvents

Room-temperature dissolution with amines like ethylenediamine and propanediamine—halogen-free, low toxicity, high selectivity.

Enzyme-Assisted Breakdown

Dual-enzyme systems with up to 10,000× activity for mixed plastic streams without degrading quality.

Energy Efficiency

3–10× lower energy consumption than thermal chemical recycling (no 400–700°C stage).

Circular Solvent Loop

95% solvent recovery through closed-loop purification and reuse for near-zero waste operations.
